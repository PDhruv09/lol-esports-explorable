Game Length Analysis

This visualization examines the distribution of game lengths in professional League of Legends matches.

What is being visualized?

The histogram represents the frequency of matches lasting various lengths of time. The dataset consists of professional matches from 2022, showing how often games reach certain durations.

Why is it being visualized?

Understanding game length trends helps teams and analysts develop strategies around early, mid, and late-game playstyles. If a league has shorter games on average, teams may prioritize aggressive early-game strategies.

How is it being visualized?

The visualization is a histogram created using D3.js. It bins game durations into intervals and displays how frequently each duration occurs, giving insight into typical match lengths in competitive play.
