Win Rate by Region

This visualization compares win rates across different competitive leagues.

What is being visualized?

This bar chart displays the average win rates of teams across various regional leagues in professional League of Legends matches.

Why is it being visualized?

Regional differences in playstyle, meta adaptations, and team performance can have a significant impact on win rates. Analyzing this data allows us to see which regions perform better on average and whether certain leagues dominate international events.

How is it being visualized?

The visualization is an interactive bar chart created using D3.js. Users can filter the data by year to examine how win rates fluctuate over time for different leagues. The x-axis represents different leagues, while the y-axis shows the average win rate as a percentage.
