Gold Differential vs. Win Probability

This visualization examines how gold advantage at a given time in the game influences win probability.

What is being visualized?

The chart represents the correlation between gold difference at the 25-minute mark and the probability of winning the match. It provides insights into whether a higher gold lead translates to a greater chance of victory.

Why is it being visualized?

Gold differential is a key indicator of a team's strength in League of Legends. Understanding how different levels of gold advantage impact win probability helps teams evaluate strategic decisions regarding fights, objectives, and risk-taking.

How is it being visualized?

The visualization uses a scatter plot in D3.js, where each point represents a match. The x-axis shows the gold difference at 25 minutes, while the y-axis represents the win probability. The color of the points indicates whether the team ultimately won or lost.
