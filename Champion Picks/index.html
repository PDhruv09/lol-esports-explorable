Champion Selection Impact

This visualization analyzes how champion selection affects win rates in professional League of Legends esports.

What is being visualized?

This chart presents win rates for champions based on their selection in matches. The data represents professional games from 2022, showcasing how often a champion is picked and their corresponding win rates.

Why is it being visualized?

Champion selection is a critical part of League of Legends strategy. Understanding which champions have the highest impact on match outcomes helps players, analysts, and teams refine their drafting strategies to improve performance.

How is it being visualized?

The visualization is an interactive bar chart using D3.js. Users can filter the chart by selecting specific champions from a dropdown menu to compare win rates. The data is processed dynamically to show how champion selection correlates with overall success.
