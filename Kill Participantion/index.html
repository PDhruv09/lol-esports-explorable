Kill Participation vs. Win Rate

This visualization explores the relationship between player kill participation and match outcomes.

What is being visualized?

The scatter plot represents kill participation percentage for individual matches and how it correlates with win rate. Kill participation is calculated based on the percentage of total team kills a player was involved in.

Why is it being visualized?

Kill participation is a key indicator of a player's contribution to their teamâ€™s performance. Analyzing this metric helps in understanding whether higher kill involvement translates to higher win rates.

How is it being visualized?

The visualization is a scatter plot created using D3.js. The x-axis represents kill participation percentage, while the y-axis represents the win rate. Data points are color-coded to indicate wins and losses, providing an interactive way to assess trends.
