Objective Control & Win Correlation

This visualization examines how controlling key objectives impacts win rates in professional League of Legends matches.

What is being visualized?

This scatter plot illustrates the correlation between securing key objectives—such as First Dragon, First Baron, and First Tower—and match outcomes.

Why is it being visualized?

Objectives play a crucial role in determining a team's chances of winning. Teams that secure early objectives gain strategic advantages, making it important to analyze how these objectives influence match results.

How is it being visualized?

The visualization uses a scatter plot in D3.js, where each data point represents a match. Users can select different objectives from a dropdown menu to observe how controlling them impacts win rates.
